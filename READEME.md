## WWWeb3 | World Wide Web3 Websites

This boilerplate introduces WWWeb3 Websites. 
Using the power of the blockchain to empower your next web project.

## Deploy
Deploy WWWeb3.sol to any EVM compatible network.

## Connect
Distribute WWWeb3.html and view your site.  

## Update
Publish changes through input field. 
1) write content
2) broadcast transaction, pay gas
3) WWWeb3 site livereloads

## Use Cases
There are many use cases for WWWeb3 sites to design, and develop further. 
Such as:
- Social media
- Live content
- Pay-to-Write
- & more!
